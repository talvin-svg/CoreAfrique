## About Core Afrique Investments Limited

Core Afrique Investments Ltd. is a wholly Ghanaian-owned consulting firm specializing in investment and financial advisory services across Africa, with a primary focus on Ghana.

We provide tailored services to offshore capital providers and local organizations, including deal flow generation for private equity, project finance structuring, feasibility studies, business plans, funding securing, and comprehensive financial advisory.

## Core Business Areas

1. Mergers and Acquisitions
2. Project Finance and Public-Private Partnerships (PPP)
3. Venture Capital Deal Flows
4. Private Equity Deals  
   - Due Diligence  
   - Preparation of Information Memoranda  
   - Transaction Advisory  
   - Financial Modeling  
   - Investment and Financial Valuation
5. Arranger/Co-Arranger for Debt Financing of Local Companies
6. Feasibility Studies and Business Plans
7. Representation of Foreign Interests in Local Companies
8. Brokering Portfolio Investments in Locally Listed Companies
